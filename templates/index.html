Common Rice Plant Diseases

Bacterial Blight

A bacterial disease that causes water-soaked lesions on leaves. It can spread rapidly in wet conditions.

  • Water-soaked lesions on leaves
  • Yellow to white streaks
  • Wilting of leaves

Brown Spot

A fungal disease that causes brown spots on leaves and grains. It can reduce yield significantly.

  • Brown spots on leaves
  • Spots on grains
  • Reduced grain quality

Leaf blast

A fungal disease that causes black powdery spots on leaves. It can affect photosynthesis and reduce yield.

  • Black powdery spots
  • Leaf discoloration
  • Reduced photosynthesis
